Quick Overview

Founded in 1976, Rocket Elevators is a privately-owned Canadian business based in Montreal,QC. From a single elevator mechanic, Rocket has grown to become one of the few fully-integrated Canadian elevator companies that engineers, manufactures, installs, services, and maintains elevating devices.

Rockets' commitment to hiring only the best staff makes us an industry leader in built-to-order elevating devices. With decades of experience, our sales staff is responsive to your needs and able to provide in-depth, pre-contract consultation (no charge) to ensure the ideal product or service for your situation

Our highly skilled fabricating staff uses state-of-the-art CNC equipment to manufacture elevators and controllers using lean manufacturing techniques, ensuring that only consistent, high-quality products bear the Rocket Elevator name.
